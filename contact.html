About Me

Hi! My name is Sunny and I'm a third year robotics engineering student at the University of Toronto. I'm currently working at the Hospital for SickKids in Toronto, Ontario under the Center for Image Guided Innovation and Therapeutic Intervention (CIGITI) where I've been developing specialized trans-oral robotic surgery tools and systems. I'm passionate about all things robotics and love doing research in the field. Outside of my work, I enjoy photography, sports, and film. Feel free to reach out at sunnyj.zhang@mail.utoronto.ca
